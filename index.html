Nature’s Grave is a top down 2D action game that integrates various combat mechanics. Inspired by other entries in the genre, players can use a combination of grapples and dashes to provide a unique experience creating that tense moment to moment action. This intensity is also reflected in the art style that distinguishes itself by having painterly, textured sprites and environments.
