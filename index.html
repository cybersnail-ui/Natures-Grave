Nature's Grave

A top-down 2D action game by the Graveyard Shift

About

Nature's Grave is a 2D top-down action game focused on intense combat interactions and evocative environments. You navigate the world as a nature spirit with the goal of defeating the blighted monsters that have poisoned the world. Fight your way through the corruption and bring life back to a dead land!

Restore a corrupt world

In a world corrupted by malice, you the player, control a nature spirit in pursuit of restoring life to the lands by defeating blighted enemies.

Face challenging monstrocities

Each blighted foe poses a unique challenge. Learn, adapt and master their playstyles on your journey.

High speed combat

Dodge attacks with your dash and use your grapple hook to escape or engage enemies.

Find yourself in a lost world

Explore the lands and gather cryptic messages of the events that took place.
